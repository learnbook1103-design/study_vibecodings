Recipes Using Chicken

Chicken is a versatile and popular ingredient, perfect for a wide range of dishes. Here are a few simple ideas to get you started.

Simple Baked Chicken Breast

Season chicken breast with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20-25 minutes until cooked through. Great for meal prep!

Quick Chicken Stir-fry

Sauté diced chicken with your favorite vegetables (broccoli, bell peppers, snap peas) and a stir-fry sauce. Serve over rice or noodles.

Chicken Salad Sandwich

Shred cooked chicken and mix with mayonnaise, celery, red onion, and a touch of mustard. Serve on bread or lettuce wraps.

One-Pan Lemon Herb Chicken and Veggies

Toss chicken pieces with potatoes, carrots, and herbs (rosemary, thyme), lemon juice, and olive oil. Roast until tender and golden.
